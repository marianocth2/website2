Frequently asked questions

Your questions about Coolizi

How does the Coolizi Portable AC work?

The Portable AC draws in warm air, passes it over cooling coils that absorb the heat, then sends cool air back into the room. The condensation evaporates inside the unit, with no exhaust hose required. In heating mode it uses PTC ceramic elements for safe, even warmth.

Is professional installation needed?

No. The Coolizi Portable AC is fully Plug & Play. Just place it where you need it, plug it in, and you immediately enjoy cool or warm air. No hoses, no drilling and no installer required.

What room size can the Portable AC cover?

The Portable AC is designed for rooms up to roughly 50m², depending on insulation and the layout of your space. It cools by up to 16°C and heats up to 45°C for year-round comfort.

How does Coolizi Cooling Ace work?

Cooling Ace uses an evaporative cooling cartridge. The water in the cartridge evaporates while a fan directs cool air towards you. According to the manufacturer, it can lower the temperature by up to 11°C in 30 seconds by focusing on the immediate space around you.

What is the difference between the two models?

The Portable AC is a reversible air conditioner (heating and cooling) for a whole room up to roughly 50m², with remote control and touchscreen. The Cooling Ace is a personal evaporative cooler, more compact and rechargeable, ideal for cooling the immediate space around you (desk, bedside).

Are they quiet and energy-efficient?

Yes. Both devices are designed for very quiet operation and low energy consumption. The Portable AC runs on low voltage to help reduce your bill, and the rechargeable Cooling Ace runs for just pennies a day.
